NATURAL MATERIALS

Wood has been specifically treated by firing it at least six times to enhance its strength and wear and tear resistance. Stone is also a natural material that is well-suited to the local environment, as it is able to retain heat during the day and release it at night. Metal not only adds to the aesthetic appeal, but is also durable and able to withstand negative impact. Overall, the use of these natural materials helps to create a sustainable and eco-friendly environment that is perfectly suited to the local climate.
