EXTERIOR

COLOURS

The grandeur and tranquility of the surrounding desert is a basis for inspiration displayed by the colours, a palette of three cool neutral tones - cream, champagne and bronze. These colors are often associated with elegance, sophistication, and serenity, making them a perfect match for the surroundings of Keturah Reserve. The use of these colors also helps to create a sense of harmony and balance, which adds to the overall relaxing atmosphere of the resort.
